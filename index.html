Paid Engagement

Authenticated Security Assessment

A comprehensive look inside your Microsoft 365 tenant and identity configuration. Requires read-only access and a signed authorization letter.

  • MFA enrollment status for all users and admin accounts
  • Admin role assignments and privileged access review
  • M365 tenant configuration against industry security benchmarks
  • OAuth consent posture — what third-party apps have access and what permissions they hold
  • Email security — forwarding rules, transport rules, delegate permissions
  • Data protection — SharePoint sharing settings, audit logging, DLP status
